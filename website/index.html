FAQ

What is TrueBlocks Key?

TrueBlocks Key is an old-fashioned Web 2.0 that solves the same problem
as TrueBlocks Core, but does so through an AWS-based Web API. This
makes it easy to use, but it also means we will capture your data and use
it for our own purposes. In that sense, it's not Web 3.0 at all. Your
call though.

What is TrueBlocks Core?

TrueBlocks Core is a 100% local-first, fully-open source EVM-compatible
blockchain indexing solution. It runs on your laptop without relience on
any third-party (including us!).

I thought TrueBlocks was anti-Web 2.0. What's up?

We were never anti- anything. We always tried to
make the the point that building the hard part (i.e. the decentralized
part) should be done first. We've done that. It's called TrueBlocks Core.

Is TrueBlocks shifting its focus away from decentralization?

No, TrueBlocks remains committed to decentralization. The introduction of TrueBlocks
Key is part of our effort to provide users with diverse options for their specific
needs. TrueBlocks Core, with its 100% local-first and decentralized nature, remains at the
heart of our commitment to empowering users with secure and independent blockchain solutions.

Is Key Decentralized?

No.

Is Core Decentralized?

100%.
